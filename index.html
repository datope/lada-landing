About the game

Your LADA.
Your rules.

Take your classic Russian Lada 2107, fully customize and upgrade every part — engine, suspension, styling, and performance.

Hit the streets to compete in high-speed races, speed traps, time trials, and more against other real Lada racers.

Beta release. LADA Racing is an early version of a much larger project — XRacing. The full version will feature more cars and a lot of other cool stuff.
